Horse Riding

I started riding Horses at a very young age and have loved it ever since. I am fortunate enough to have competed at All Ireland level. I competed with the Duhallow Pony Club both in teams and individually. In recent years I have decided to stop competing and ride more for leisure. I am lucky enough to continue to have horses during college thanks to having a family all interested in horses. I am also a member of the UCC Equestrian Club which is a great way to make friends with similar interests.



Rugby

Even though I don't play rugby myself I have a keen interest in following this sport. I watch all the Six Nations matches and have a family who has a huge interest in this sport. As a transition year student I took a course that qualifies me to train rugby up until a u13 level.



Basketball

I used to play basketball for the club in Mallow. We won the county final in 2015 and got into the all Ireland play offs. I would love to go back playing basketball in the future.
